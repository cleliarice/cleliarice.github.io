Ruwana is a local initiative based in Arequipa, Peru. Our mission is to empower women by providing fair work opportunities while creating beautiful knitted and crocheted items. Every cardigan, top, bag, and accessory is handcrafted with care by our talented moms. Your support helps families thrive while bringing one-of-a-kind handmade products into your life.
