Ruwana

Our Mission

Ruwana is a local initiative in Arequipa, Peru. We empower moms by creating opportunities to make handcrafted knitted and crocheted products, turning their talent into sustainable income.

The Moms Behind Ruwana

Every product tells a story — the story of a skilled mom who invests love and care into her craft. By supporting Ruwana, you’re supporting them and their families.
